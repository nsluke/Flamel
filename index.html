Is this a scam?
Absolutely not. We are a pre-revenue, pre-product, pre-reactor, pre-regulatory-approval deep tech startup. That's completely different. We have a pitch deck and everything.
Can you actually make gold?
Technically, yes! CERN produced 29 picograms over four years. That's 0.000000000029 grams. At current gold prices, that's worth approximately $0.000000002. We intend to scale this by mass-producing fusion reactors, which also don't commercially exist yet.
Why is the gold radioactive?
Think of it as a feature, not a bug. Our gold comes with built-in beta radiation — perfect for cancer treatment, impractical for jewelry, and absolutely devastating for your Geiger counter. We like to say it has "energy."

Our current roadmap targets first delivery in 2044, accounting for reactor construction (5-10 years), NRC licensing (150,000 person-hours), and the mandatory 14-18 year radioactive sequestration period. We move fast and break atomic bonds.
